Critical & Major Gaps

1 item
GAP AC-1.8.4 LLM Provider Unavailable Banner Missing

Spec requires: When LLM provider is completely unavailable (NFR-008), an error banner displays at the top of the drawer indicating temporary unavailability, and the chat input is disabled with a visual indicator.

Actual: Only a single inline error mechanism exists (errorState/errorType) that renders in the input zone. There is no separate "provider unavailable" state, no banner at the top of the drawer, and no mechanism to disable chat input independently of a per-message error.

Impact: Users cannot distinguish between a single failed message and a systemic outage. Without a top-of-drawer banner and disabled input, they'll keep trying to send messages during an outage.
